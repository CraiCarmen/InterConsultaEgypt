About us



As time is essence in every aspect in our life, InterConsulta Swiss Company launched its activities since 1964 in Switzerland. InterConsulta Swiss was the core Company in consultation business and became the cornerstone for other Companies, institutions as well as individuals for their success, and for new emerged entities.

The Company's loyal clients around the world are witnessing this with their success stories. The administrative owners are provided with professional experts and employees in all business fields.

In 1983, The Parent company targeted Egypt to form its branch InterConsulta Egypt. During this time the country strived to rebuild its infrastructure and was in need of consultancy experts.

The company carried on its works until end of December 1999, and had a new launching at the end of 2017.

The Company specializes in managing wealth, and financial consultation service. Interconsulta Egypt prepared a study about the Egyptian market and its requirements, taking into account the country leading role in economy after years of economic degeneracy.

Our history



At a time when everything seems to be spinning faster, we are a constant partner. Nevertheless, since the founding of InterConsulta in 1964, we have never stopped. But, rather, we have reinvented ourselves time and time again and have moved forward with our clients.

The best proof of this is our customers, some of whom have been loyal to us for decades and over several generations. We started in Egypt since 1984 to 1999 and back again in 2017.
