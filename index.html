About Us

Established originally in Switzerland in 1964, InterConsulta Egypt remains a premier institutional standard-bearer for operational execution and corporate growth strategy. Establishing operations in Egypt in 1983, the enterprise became directly involved in core national industrial infrastructure pathways.
