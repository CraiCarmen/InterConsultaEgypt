About Us

Established originally in Switzerland in 1964, InterConsulta Egypt remains a premier institutional standard-bearer for operational execution and corporate growth strategy. Establishing operations in Egypt in 1983, the enterprise became directly involved in core national industrial infrastructure pathways.

Following a corporate relaunch in 2017, the firm specifically channels high-level portfolio wealth management, asset verification metrics, and specialized structural consulting for multinational entities.

Our History

Decades of unbroken cross-border trust and generational client retainment.

In a business ecosystem shaped by rapid technological shifts and unpredictable regulatory transitions, InterConsulta stands as a steady, reliable anchor. Since 1964, we have never stopped innovating, constantly updating our delivery platforms alongside our corporate network.

The definitive verification of our success lies in our client longevity—many of our premier institutional partners have retained our consultation tracks through multi-generational successions. We established strong operational branches in Egypt from 1984 to 1999, renewing our nationwide expansion under our optimized 2017 baseline framework.
