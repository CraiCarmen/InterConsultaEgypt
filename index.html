About us

Founded in Switzerland in 1964, InterConsulta Egypt is a global pillar of business excellence, empowering institutions and individuals for decades. Since expanding to Egypt in 1983, the firm has been instrumental in infrastructure development and economic growth.

Following a strategic relaunch in 2017,InterConsulta Egypt now specializes in expert wealth management and financial consultancy. By leveraging deep market analysis and a team of professional experts, they provide tailored solutions for the modern Egyptian economy.
With a legacy of loyalty and success stories worldwide, InterConsulta Egypt remains a trusted partner in securing your financial future.

Our history


At a time when everything seems to be spinning faster, we are a constant partner. Nevertheless, since the founding of InterConsulta Egypt in 1964, we have never stopped. But, rather, we have reinvented ourselves time and time again and have moved forward with our clients.
