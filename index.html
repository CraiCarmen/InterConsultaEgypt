About Us

Established originally in Switzerland in 1964, InterConsulta Egypt remains a premier institutional standard-bearer for operational execution and corporate growth strategy. Establishing operations in Egypt in 1983, the enterprise became directly involved in core national industrial infrastructure pathways.

Following a corporate relaunch in 2017, the firm channels high-level portfolio wealth management, asset verification metrics, and specialized structural consulting for multinational entities.

Institutional Standards

Utilizing precise regional index assessments, risk analysis architecture, and an elite team of senior legal and financial specialists, we supply custom target frameworks to address the modern dynamics of the Egyptian macroeconomy.
